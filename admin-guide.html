2. Managing Tournaments

2.1 Creating a New Tournament

Step 1: Navigate to Tournaments Tab

Click on the TOURNAMENTS tab in the navigation menu.

Step 2: Click "Create Tournament"

Click the green "Create Tournament" button in the top right.

Step 3: Fill in Tournament Details

  • Tournament Name: e.g., "The Legs Open 2026"
  • Year: Tournament year
  • Start Date: When the tournament begins
  • End Date: When the tournament ends
  • Status: Choose from Upcoming, Active, or Completed

Step 4: Search for Golf Course (Optional)

  • Enter course name in the search box (minimum 3 characters)
  • Click "Search Courses"
  • Select from search results
  • Choose a tee (e.g., Championship, Men's, Ladies')
  • Course details will be automatically populated

Step 5: Manual Course Entry (Alternative)

If you can't find the course, manually enter:

  • Course name
  • Slope rating (default: 113)
  • Course rating (default: 72)

Step 6: Save Tournament

Click "Create Tournament" to save.

2.2 Setting the Active Tournament

The active tournament is what users see by default when they log in.

To Set a Tournament as Active:

  1. Go to the TOURNAMENTS tab
  2. Find the tournament you want to activate
  3. Click the "Set as Active" button
Important: Only one tournament can be active at a time. Setting a new tournament as active will automatically deactivate the previous one.

2.3 Deleting a Tournament

‚ö†Ô∏è Warning: Deleting a tournament is permanent and will delete all associated data including groups, scores, and player assignments. This action cannot be undone!

To Delete a Tournament:

  1. Navigate to the TOURNAMENTS tab
  2. Click the red "Delete" button (trash icon) on the tournament
  3. Confirm the deletion in the popup dialog

3. Managing Course Details

3.1 Editing Tournament Information

Steps:

  1. Select a tournament from the TOURNAMENTS tab
  2. Navigate to the COURSE tab
  3. Click "Edit Tournament"
  4. Modify tournament name, year, dates, or status
  5. Click "Save Changes"

3.2 Editing Course Information

Steps:

  1. On the COURSE tab, click "Edit Course"
  2. Update course name, slope rating, or course rating
  3. Click "Save Changes"

3.3 Editing Hole Details

To Edit Par and Stroke Index:

  1. On the COURSE tab, scroll to the hole details table
  2. Click "Edit Holes"
  3. Click on any par or stroke index cell to edit
  4. Enter new values
  5. Click "Save Changes"

4. Player Management

4.1 Adding a New Player to the System

Steps:

  1. Go to the PLAYERS tab
  2. Click "Add Player"
  3. Fill in player details:
    • Name: Player's full name
    • Handicap: Current handicap (required)
    • CDH Number: Club Disability Handicap number (optional)
    • Bio: Player biography (optional)
    • Photo URL: Link to player photo (optional)
  4. Click "Add Player"

4.2 Viewing Player Profiles

Steps:

  1. Navigate to the PLAYERS tab
  2. Click on any player card to view their full profile
  3. View tournament history, including:
    • Final position (e.g., "Finished 3rd of 15 players")
    • Gross and net scores
    • Stableford points
  4. Click "View Scorecard" to see hole-by-hole scores

5. Tournament Setup

5.1 Adding Players to a Tournament

Method 1: Add Existing Players

  1. Select the tournament from TOURNAMENTS tab
  2. Go to the SETUP tab
  3. Scroll to "Add Existing Players" section
  4. Click "Add" next to each player you want to include

Method 2: Create and Add New Player

  1. On the SETUP tab, find "Add New Player" section
  2. Enter player name and handicap
  3. Click "Add Player"
  4. Player will be automatically added to the tournament

5.2 Creating Groups - Random Method

Requirement: You need at least 4 players to create groups.

Steps:

  1. On the SETUP tab, find "Create Groups" section
  2. Click "Random Groups"
  3. Players will be randomly shuffled into groups of 4
  4. Each group is assigned:
    • A unique 4-digit PIN code
    • A scorer (first player in the group)
    • A group number

5.3 Creating Groups - Manual Method

Steps:

  1. On the SETUP tab, click "Manual Groups"
  2. You'll see an available players pool and group cards
  3. Drag and drop players from the pool into groups
  4. For each group:
    • Enter a custom group name (e.g., "Morning Flight", "Championship Flight")
    • Enter a tee time (e.g., "09:00")
  5. Use "Add Group" or "Remove Group" buttons to adjust number of groups
  6. When done, click "Save Groups"
  7. Click "Cancel" to discard changes
Validation: The system will ensure all players are assigned to a group before allowing you to save.

5.4 Managing Group PINs

Each group receives a unique 4-digit PIN code for score entry.

Viewing Group PINs:

  1. On the SETUP tab, scroll to "Groups" section
  2. Each group card displays its PIN code
  3. Share these PINs with the respective group scorers
Security: Group PINs are only visible to admins. Players with a group PIN can only enter scores for their own group.

6. Score Management

6.1 Entering Scores

Steps:

  1. Navigate to the SCORING tab
  2. Select a group from the dropdown
  3. For each player and hole:
    • Enter the number of strokes
    • Press Enter or Tab to move to next field
  4. Scores are automatically saved
Handicap Calculations: The system automatically calculates:
  • Playing handicap based on slope rating
  • Net scores (gross - handicap strokes per hole)
  • Stableford points

6.2 Understanding Score Display

Score cells are color-coded for easy reading:

Score Type Color Meaning
Eagle or better Gold background 2+ under par
Birdie Light green background 1 under par
Par White background Equal to par
Bogey Light orange background 1 over par
Double bogey or worse Light red background 2+ over par
